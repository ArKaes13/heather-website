Parmesan Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • ⅓ cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 575 degrees.
  2. Mix ingredients and shape into 12 meatballs.
  3. Place on a greased foil-covered baking sheet.
  4. Bake 25 min or until done.
