Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients

To Marinate

  • 12 oz chicken breast
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon shaoxing wine (sub mirin)
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of white pepper

Sauce

  • 1 Tbsp light soy sauce
  • ½ tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 Tbsp water
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

The rest

  • 3 Tbsp oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic (smashed and sliced)
  • 2 thin slices of ginger(minced)
  • 2 dried red chilies(deseeded and chopped, adjust for spicyness)
  • ½ tsp Sichuan peppercorn powder
  • 6 scallions (white portions only)
  • 1 red and green bell pepper
  • ½ white onion
    • <1i>1 cup of roasted peanuts

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken breast into ¾ inch cubes. Mix together the chicken and all the marinade ingredients into a bowl and set aside for 20 minutes.
  2. Mix together all the sauce ingredients into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Heat 2 Tbsp of oil in a wok/pan over high heat, sear the chicken and then remove from the wok/pan and set aside.
  4. Turn the heat to low and add another Tbsp of oil. Add the garlic, ginger, chilies, Sichuan peppercorn powder, and scallions. Cook for a minute or two until fragrant.
  5. Add the chicken back to the wok/pan and turn up to high heat. Stir-fry for a minute and then stir the sauce and add to the wok/pan. Stir-fry for another minute, the sauce should thicken up quickly.
  6. Finally, add the peanuts. Give everything a final stir and serve.
