Wonton Soup

Ingredients

Broth:

  • 16 large wonton/dumplings or 24 medium wonton. Or use more or less as needed.
  • 32 ounce/ 4 cups Vegetable Broth
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 tsp Ginger, minced
  • 1 Garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce, light
  • ½ tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tsp Hot Chili Oil/ Sambel Olek
  • Salt & Peppe as needed

Garnish & Toppings:

  • ½ cup Cabbage, shredded
  • ½ cup Carrots, matchstick cut
  • 5 to 6 Green Onions/Scallions, cut diagonally
  • Few wedges of Lime
  • Light Soy Sauce & Hot Chili Oil on the side for personalizing

Instructions

Preparing Broth:

  1. First of all, place inner pot inside the Instant Pot. Plug it in.
  2. Further, add vegetable stock, water, minced ginger and garlic, rice vinegar, light soy sauce, sesame oil, and hot chili oil to the inner pot.
  3. Give it a good stir. Place the lid on it and close it. Finally, set the valve on SEALING. Pressure cook on HIGH for 3 minutes.
  4. Most noteworthy is that it does not matter if you do QPR or follow NPR. If you are in rush, then perform QPR or follow NPR.
  5. The BROTH is ready.
  6. Certainly you can prepare it ahead of time for your meals.
  7. It can be heated at the time of serving on SAUTE and then served. You can easily double the recipe. This broth can be frozen.

Making Wonton Soup:

  1. Please note that this is a crucial step.
  2. You have to cook Vegetable Frozen Wonton in the broth right before serving.
  3. Therefore this is what you do - Open the lid. Firstly, put the broth on SAUTE MODE. Once it comes to the boil, finally add frozen vegetable wonton to it. Cook it for 2-3 minutes till done.
  4. Immediately serve in the Soup Bowls. Garnish and Slurrp.

Garnish the Soup:

  1. Top the soup with shredded cabbage, carrot matchsticks, diagonally cut green onions. Finally, give it a good squeeze of lime juice.
  2. Add more soy sauce and hot chili oil as needed. I always do.
  3. The vegetables on the top add great crunch and texture to the soup.
  4. Please note that if you like your vegetables slightly soft, then boil these vegetables with the wonton.
